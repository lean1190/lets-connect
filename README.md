## Let's connect
A simple and effective way to stay in touch with your entrepreneurial circle.

- Shaping entrepreneurial circles is hard.
- Sometimes, we are forced to remember "what did we connect for?".
- After connecting with someone, it can be hard to find them again.

#### This app solves the struggle.
